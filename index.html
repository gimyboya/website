We Love Helping The Community

MTCAS is a FREE system that will assist you to make better decisions on the land use types for a better crop decision.

Collection of data about land quality over time and space has outperformed human ability to interpret and apply them to check the land use types. Sometimes that data is mismatched and land use types that are not suitable with soil properties are associated to a land and this land will be planted, which leads to a cease of grow for the crops and the crops will wither away. This would consequently waste a lot of time, resources and money and could affect the country economy badly. The site-specific management has lagged as a result of poor agronomic interpretation and information delivery.

Our CEO
